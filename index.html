Privacy Policy Focus Nest

Last Updated: April 20.04, 2026

1. Data Controller

The data controller is Joanna Maleszka, an independent developer based in Poland, operating the Focus Nest application (“Controller”).

Contact: app.focusnest@gmail.com

2. Overview and "No-Account" Policy

Focus Nest is a “Privacy First” application. Focus Nest does not require user accounts. No personal profiles are created or stored on our servers.

3. Data Collection and App Permissions

3.1 Personal Data: We do not collect name, email, or phone number.

3.2 Local Data & Technologies: All sessions and settings are stored locally on your device (SharedPreferences, WebView Local Storage). Only you can access this data.

3.2.1 System Backups: If Android system backup is enabled on your device, local App data may be included in your encrypted Google account backup. The Controller has no access to these backups.

3.3 Android Permissions:

  • Foreground Service: Keep the timer active in background sessions.
  • Exact Alarms: Trigger session completion at the precise time.
  • Wake Lock & Full Screen Intent: Display completion screen on locked devices.
  • Disable Keyguard: Allow stopping alarms without unlocking.
  • Notifications: Deliver alerts and timers when a session ends.
  • Vibration: Provide haptic feedback for timer events.
  • Network & Internet: Verify purchases via RevenueCat and display personalized ads.

3.4 Technical Data & Identifiers: We process device model, OS version, and App usage statistics. Usage statistics (such as session duration and integrity scores) are processed locally to provide you with in-app analytics and remain on your device. Third-party SDKs (AdMob, RevenueCat) process pseudonymous identifiers (AAID) and purchase tokens for fraud prevention and purchase validation only.

4. Third-Party Services & International Transfers

The App uses third-party services including:

  • Google (Google Play Services, AdMob) – Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat (Purchase validation and licensing) – Privacy Policy

Data may be transferred outside the European Economic Area, including to the United States. Where required, transfers rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and/or the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework.

5. Advertising Disclosure (AdMob)

We display personalized ads through Google AdMob. This involves:

  • Advertising ID (AAID)
  • IP address
  • Device identifiers
  • Approximate location derived from IP address by Google AdMob.
  • Ad interactions and impressions
  • Fraud prevention signals
  • Limited diagnostics collected automatically

Consent is requested via Google UMP (IAB TCF 2.2) for users in the EU. Users can opt out via device settings or Ad Preferences.

6. AI & Automated Decisions (EU AI Act)

Focus Nest does not use your data for AI model training or automated decision-making affecting you legally.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 (16 in EU). No personal data is knowingly collected. If discovered, we delete it immediately.

8. Legal Basis (GDPR)

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) – for personalized advertising.
  • Contractual Necessity (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) – to provide and validate Elite premium features.
  • Legitimate Interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) – for app functionality, fraud prevention, and security.

9. User Rights & Data Deletion

You may access, correct, or delete your data:

  • Right to be Forgotten: Clear App storage or uninstall the App.
  • Data Portability: Export not supported at this time.
  • California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA): We do not sell personal data. Request access, deletion, or opt-out at app.focusnest@gmail.com.

10. Data Retention

Local App data is retained on your device until deletion or uninstall. No personal data is stored on our servers. Third-party services retain only necessary purchase and advertising data per their policies.

11. RevenueCat Disclosure

RevenueCat processes purchase receipts and transaction identifiers solely for license validation and fraud prevention. No credit card information is collected or stored by the Controller. RevenueCat acts as a data processor under GDPR.

12. Data Security

We use Android security mechanisms (sandboxing, encryption). Data transmitted to third parties is protected using HTTPS/TLS encryption.

13. Changes to this Policy

Updates may occur. Continued App use indicates acceptance. Latest version available online at: appfocusnest.github.io/privacy/.
