Privacy Policy Focus Nest

Last Updated: April 19, 2026

1. Data Controller

The data controller is Joanna Maleszka, an independent developer based in Poland, operating the Focus Nest application (“Controller”).

Contact: app.focusnest@gmail.com

2. Overview and "No-Account" Policy

Focus Nest is a “Privacy First” application. Focus Nest does not require user accounts. No personal profiles are created or stored on our servers. Some limited processing of user-related data may occur through third-party services integrated into the App.

3. Data Collection and App Permissions

3.1 Personal Data: We do not directly collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or phone number. However, third-party services used in the App (such as advertising and purchase providers) may collect device identifiers, usage data, and diagnostic information.

3.2 Local Data & Technologies: App-generated data (such as sessions and settings) is stored locally on your device, excluding data processed by third-party services.

3.3 Android Permissions:

  • Foreground Service: Keep the timer active in background sessions.
  • Exact Alarms: Trigger session completion at the precise time.
  • Wake Lock & Full Screen Intent: Display completion screen on locked devices.
  • Notifications: Deliver alerts and timers when a session ends.
  • Network & Internet: Verify purchases via RevenueCat and display personalized ads.

3.4 Role of Third-Party Providers: Third-party service providers integrated into the App (such as Google and RevenueCat) act as independent data controllers or processors. Their collection and use of data are governed by their respective privacy policies.

4. Advertising Disclosure (AdMob)

We display personalized ads through Google AdMob. This involves: Advertising ID (AAID), IP address, and Device identifiers. This data is used for advertising personalization, fraud prevention, and analytics purposes as defined by Google advertising policies. Consent is requested via Google UMP for users in the EU.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Since the App does not require user accounts and does not store data on external servers, uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data. Third-party service providers retain data according to their own retention policies.

6. Legal Basis (GDPR)

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) – for personalized advertising.
  • Contractual Necessity (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) – to provide and validate Elite premium features.
  • Third-Party Processing: Advertising data is processed by third-party providers under their own privacy policies.
